A maximal diameter theorem for metric measure spaces
with positive Ricci curvature bounded below

Christian Ketterer, University of Cologne

We will survey rigidity theorems under positive lower Ricci curvature bounds in the context of metric measure spaces. This will include a maximal diameter theorem and some of its consequences like the sphere theorem or Obata's theorem. I outline key steps and ideas of the proofs. In particular I explain how Euclidean metric cones area useful tool therein.
